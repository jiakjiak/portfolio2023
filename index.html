Hi, I am Jia Koh

My journey

I am a San Francisco-based project manager who helps Fortune 500 companies streamline processes and improve service delivery. Originally from Singapore, I am passionate about immigrant advocacy, innovation, and leading with integrity and excellence.

Boeing
Service Manager, Crew Ops & Service Management
Feb 2020 - Oct 2020

My tenure with Boeing was the most exciting and challenging in my aviation career. As a former end-user of Jeppesen, I learned first-hand how software correlates to quality of life. It became my mantra to champion end-user experience and enlighten my team on flight crew matters. Given my customer experience background, I established myself as a subject matter expert in the field, delivering sessions on service excellence.

At the height of the pandemic, my colleagues and I joined forces to build a prototype for the airline frontline workers to improve their working conditions. Tapping on each other's expertise, our innovative spirit won us second place in the Boeing hackathon.

I was privileged to be part of an exceptional team. I would not trade this experience for the world.

Northumbria University
B.A.(Hon) in Business with International Management
Sep 2017 - Jun 2019

Returning to school was one of my biggest life achievements because I am the first university graduate in my family. Throughout the undergraduate program, my incredible lecturers believed in my abilities and constantly challenged me intellectually.

Nominated as team leader in all the projects I was involved in, I rediscovered the importance of inspiring others through thought leadership and encouraging students to explore lateral thinking. On the other hand, I benefited from the diversity because collaborations with students from different specializations shed new insights and spurred innovative ideas. This allowed me to exercise my strategic thinking muscle.

With dedication and inspiration from my peers, I graduated with First Class Honours.

Having a B.A. opened doors and led me to advanced opportunities.

Emirates
Team Lead, Service Delivery
Sep 2011 - Aug 2017

Landing the highly-coveted role at Emirates was a dream come true and life-changing. The opportunity broadened my horizons, strengthened my workplace adaptability, and advanced my communications skills.

I fast-tracked my career to a supervisory level and was tasked with delivering and improving the quality of customer experience programs and operations while upholding airline safety. Under my care, my teams went above and beyond. By August 2017, I had received 17 recognitions for service excellence and effective team management.

On continuous improvement, I presented business solutions to Service Delivery, Product Development, and Flight Catering departments to reduce costs and streamline workplace operations. In May 2017, a product proposal became my biggest accomplishment when it came to fruition. It was implemented across all fleets, reducing premium product wastage by 0.6%.

Emirates gave me the chance to work alongside multinational colleagues and serve stakeholders from all walks of life. But I knew my time with the airline would soon come to an end. Higher education was in sight.
